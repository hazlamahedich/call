# Walkthrough: Knowledge Hub Dashboard Generation

I have generated the **Knowledge Hub** dashboard in Stitch, designed for the General Chatbot Mode.

## Design Highlights
- **Aesthetic**: Follows the "Hyper-Luminous Observer" (Mantis Cybernetics) design system.
- **Visuals**: Features signature glassmorphism, #131318 background, and teal (#00f5d4) glowing accents.
- **Key Widgets**:
    - **Knowledge Effectiveness**: Multi-series bar chart for 'Answered' vs 'Missed' queries.
    - **Knowledge Gap**: Identifies missing documentation for trending topics.
    - **Sentiment Trend**: Glowing line chart tracking customer sentiment.
    - **Usage & Costs**: Snapshot of AI compute spend.
